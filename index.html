Workflow

Design one decision and one view at a time.

  1. DiscoverClarify purpose, actors, boundaries, constraints, and priorities.
  2. Plan coverageSelect only the views that materially improve the design.
  3. Design iterativelyAsk focused questions, draft one view, review, and revise.
  4. Document consistentlyKeep source DSL, decisions, assumptions, and generated diagrams together.
