# Assumptions

Use this file to record provisional statements that allow design work to continue while information is incomplete.

Assumptions must never be presented as confirmed facts.

## Status values

- `open` — not yet confirmed or rejected
- `confirmed` — accepted as a fact by the user or an authoritative source
- `rejected` — explicitly disproved or declined
- `superseded` — replaced by a newer assumption or decision

## Review rules

- Record every assumption that affects scope, boundaries, integrations, data, behavior, deployment, security, cost, or quality attributes.
- Prefer a small number of explicit assumptions over many hidden assumptions.
- Ask the user to confirm high-impact assumptions before approving dependent views.
- When an assumption becomes confirmed, update the relevant discovery summary, terminology, decisions, and views.
- When rejected, identify every dependent view or decision that must be revised.
